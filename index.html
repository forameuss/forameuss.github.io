Dragon's Ruin

2.5D platformer game.

Aula Arcade’s course final project, where I took part as one of the programmers.

Among other things, I made the player and enemies movement, and their behaviour as well. My main goal was to achieve a good movement feeling through a custom physics system.

I also did the soundtrack and the sound design behind all the elements of the game.

You can play it here.

Also, you can listen to the original soundtrack here.

  • Date: 2018
  • Roles: Programmer, Composer & Sound Designer

Other Projects


Bored Room (2018)

Our submission for Aula Arcade Game Jam 5, where I took part as the programmer. Inspired by Angry Birds, we used 2D physics to create an engaging gameplay with challenging levels. You can play it here.

It Runs (2018)

Our submission for Aula Arcade Game Jam 6.
It’s a player vs gamemaster runner inspired by Stephen King’s “IT”, which consists of a player trying to reach as far as he can while the other player tries to stop him through traps, using a store system.
I made the programming part and most of the sounds effects.
You can play it here.
