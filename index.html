About

Alberto Navarro also known as Forameuss is a spanish composer and SFX designer who tries to find the essence of every project and fuel it in order to achieve the most powerful emotional impact through music and sound.

His background as a programmer leads him to places where music and technology intertwine, something ideal for creating interactive experiences like video games, with an enhanced audio feel.
